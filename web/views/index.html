About

One person. Full stack. No middlemen.

Est. 2025 · Netherlands
Founder · Jop Luysterburg

Eleven Web Solutions is a one-person studio based in the Netherlands. I work directly with founders, small teams, and individuals who want something built properly — not templated, not outsourced, not rushed.

By day, I work at Nationale Nederlanden — one of the Netherlands' largest financial institutions — where I was brought in to modernise their technology standards. I introduce new tech stacks, elevate IT architecture quality, and build real services used by development teams across the organisation. I know what good looks like at scale. Eleven Web Solutions is where I bring that same standard to your project — whatever the size.
